Gllacy

Gllacy is a small family shop with natural ice cream. The menu item "Creamy" leads you on the catalog page with cue masks over the items. There's also a colorful slider on the main page done with css only. Feedback form button opens a modal window with a bit of animation.

Sedona

Sedona is an advertising page of a small town in Arizona. The menu item "Hotels" leads you on the catalog page with list of hotels. "Search hotels" button opens a modal window

Device

Device is an electronic goods store with two gorgeous sliders. The "Monopods for selfie" item leds you on the catalog page with item hover-masks

Pixel-Hunter

Pixel-Hunter is a game, you need to guess if it's a picture or photo.
